Altay Firat

Altay Firat was born in 1968 in a small village of Cyprus and has completed his primary, secondary and high school studies there. Later on, he has moved to Istanbul in order to follow his educational goals. In 1990, he received his degree in Geography at Istanbul University. In 1998, he earned his master's degree in business at European University of Lefke. In the year of 2013, he obtained his PhD in Environment Education and Administration & Management at Near East University. Under the ministry of education, Altay Firat served as a teacher, deputy headmaster and headmaster between the years of 1992 and 2016 in various schools. Moreover, he is one of the co-writer of the Geography of Cyprus lecture book which is being studied by high school students. From 2016 to 2018, he has been appointed as the head of the forestry department. He worked as a part-time lecturer in various universities and has written numerous articles regarding environment and geography which have been published by well-known international journals.
